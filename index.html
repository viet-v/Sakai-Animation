The Origin and
Significance of Sakai

The Sakai brand narrative is deeply rooted in the very meaning of this Malay word.


The Sakai brand narrative is deeply rooted in the very meaning of this Malay word. It alludes to being a member of a tribe, and the tribal values of unity, loyalty and kinship together form the cornerstone of the brand philosophy.

The foundation of the Sakai philosophy is constituted on a singular belief - indeed a conviction - that the core objective of business is not merely to create short-term profit for shareholders, but to affect long-term value creation for all, including society at large, through the upliftment of the communities around us.
